Location --> Thiruvananthpuram, Kerala, India

Sree Padmanabhaswamy temple

The Padmanabhaswamy temple is a Hindu Temple located in thiruvananthpuram,the state capital of kerala. The name of the city of Thiruvananthpuram in malyalam translates to"The city of lord ananta",(The city of Lord Vishnu)reffering to the deity of the Padmanabhaswamy temple.

Location --> Srirangam, Tiruchirapalli, Tamil Nadu, India

Sree Ranganathswamy Temple

Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Ranganatha, a form of the Supreme God, Maha Vishnu, located in Srirangam, Tiruchirapalli, Tamil Nadu, India.

Location --> Puri, Orrisa, India

Sree Jagannath Temple

The Jagannath Temple is an important Hindu temple dedicated to Jagannath, a form of Sri Krishna in Puri in the state of Odisha on the eastern coast of India.

Location --> Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, India

Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga

Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingams, shrines which are said to be the most sacred abodes of Lord Shiva. It is located in the ancient city of Ujjain in the state of Madhya Pradesh, India.
